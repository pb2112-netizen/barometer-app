Privacy Policy

Baro · Last updated: June 2026

Baro does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app requires no sign-in and does not use location, contacts, or advertising identifiers.

Network

The app downloads a public result file over HTTPS from a trusted host (e.g. barometer_pl.json depending on your selected country lens). No information about you or your device is sent with that request.

Local storage

Your settings (country lens, notification threshold, notifications on/off) and the last fetched result are stored on your device only (DataStore Preferences).

Permissions

  • Internet — to fetch a public barometer file for your selected country lens (e.g. barometer_pl.json)
  • Notifications (Android 13+) — alerts are generated locally on your phone; we do not use server push notifications

Third-party content

Scores and summaries are generated automatically by AI on a server from public RSS feeds (see Content & sources in the app). They may contain errors.

Automated content

Scores and short summaries are produced automatically from public RSS headlines (including BBC News, Al Jazeera, and The Guardian World). Processing uses AI on a server — not on your device. Baro is not affiliated with these publishers. Content is for informational purposes only and is not a substitute for original reporting.

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy; the in-app text reflects the version shipped with the app.
